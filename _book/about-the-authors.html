12.2 Veerle van Son

Veerle created her Twitter account in 2012, then barely used it for six years. She only became an active Twitter user in 2018, longing to be part of the #rstats Twitter goodness and to participate in #TidyTuesday. Her data blog can be found at SurroundedByData.netlify.com. Veerle lives in Den Bosch, the Netherlands and is co-founder of R-Ladies Den Bosch.
